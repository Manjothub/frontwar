Ralph Roberts

Personal trainer Ralph Roberts was born and raised in Cherryville, North Carolina. Ralph is a nationally certified fitness trainer (CFT) and Specialist in Fitness Nutrition through ISSA. Having worked out at gyms all over the country, Ralph noticed that most people were too timid, uncomfortable or embarrassed to ask for help. Through personal training, Ralph hopes to change that mindset. Since Oct 2013
